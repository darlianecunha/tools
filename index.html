🛠️ Text Mining Assistant

A tool designed to analyse text within PDF files, identifying and highlighting up to three keywords. Results can be exported to Word and Excel, facilitating the organisation of evidence in academic and technical reports.

📊 Global Maritime Trade Dashboard

An interactive tool to analyse cargo flows in the Brazilian waterway sector, broken down by country and product type. The dashboard offers insights into international trade, revealing geographical distribution and volume trends.

⚓ Port Operations Dashboard

Interactive dashboard for analysing and visualising cargo movements in Brazilian ports, offering a comprehensive view of port activity. It helps identify logistics trends, bottlenecks, and opportunities for improvement.

🚢 Port Exports

This tool provides an overview of maritime exports handled by the port sector in 2023. The dashboard details export volumes by country and product, helping users understand trade flows and cargo distribution.
